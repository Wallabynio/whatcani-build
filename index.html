The practice

Founded on expertise, built for results.

whatcani.build was built by practitioners who saw too many good projects fail early — not because the sites were wrong, but because the planning advice arrived too late. We exist to fix that.

Istvan Bakos
Principal — Planning & Feasibility

Istvan leads the planning and feasibility side of the practice, with a background spanning architecture and urban design before moving into planning strategy. He has spent his career at the early, high-stakes point in a project — the decisions made before a single drawing is finalised — and built whatcani.build around the conviction that good planning advice should arrive before the money is spent, not after. He works directly with developers and builders on zoning strategy, feasibility, and the policy arguments that get good projects through council.

Nathan Evans
Principal — Design & Approvals

Nathan brings the design-literate half of the practice — reading plans, sections, and massing fluently enough to catch planning issues while a scheme can still be changed cheaply. Before co-founding whatcani.build, he worked across residential and mixed-use projects managing the DA and CDC process end to end, from first lodgement through to determination. He leads pre-design and approvals work, translating planning controls into briefs that architects can actually design to, and planning reports that councils can actually approve.
